Read this. Choose a word from the box. Write the correct word next to number 1–5. There is one example.
Dogs don’t live in a zoo, like tigers or elephants. They live in our homes, and they are our (1) friends ! Dogs can learn words. We can tell them to sit, stand and catch a (2) ball that we throw. Dogs can swim too, and some of them like swimming in the (3) sea . Many dogs get very (4) dirty in the park, but we give them a (5) bath to make them clean again. Dogs are funny!
