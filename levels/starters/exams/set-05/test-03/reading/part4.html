Reading & Writing, Part 4
Read this. Choose a word from the box. Write the correct word next to number 1–5. There is one example.
Crocodiles are like snakes with legs, but their babies come from (1) eggs — like birds' babies! They eat meat, and their favourite food is the (2) fish swimming in the water. They don't like running, because their (3) legs are short, but they can swim. They can see at night, because they have very good (4) eyes . Many crocodiles sleep with one eye, or their big (5) mouth , open. That's scary!
