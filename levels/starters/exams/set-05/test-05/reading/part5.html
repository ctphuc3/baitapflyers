Reading & Writing, Part 5
Look at the pictures and read the questions. Write one-word answer.
Examples
How many children can you see? four
Where are they? in the park
Questions
1 Who is with them? a
✓ Correct! Answer: dog
✗ Correct answer: dog
Dịch: Ai đang ở cùng họ?
2 Where is the dog now? in front of a
✓ Correct! Answer: table
✗ Correct answer: table
Dịch: Con chó bây giờ ở đâu?
3 What are the children doing? football
✓ Correct! Answer: playing
✗ Correct answer: playing
Dịch: Những đứa trẻ đang làm gì?
4 Where is the dog standing on? the
✓ Correct! Answer: table
✗ Correct answer: table
Dịch: Con chó đang đứng trên cái gì?
5 What is it doing? the food
✓ Correct! Answer: eating
✗ Correct answer: eating
